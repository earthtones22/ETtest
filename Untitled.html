Miss Expanding Universe Syllabus Uncreative writing. Non-editing. The Art of copying. Found poetry. Primal authenticity. Vexxed queerness. Imperfectionism. Life as collage. Uncomfortable conversations as research. Question your identity. Lose track of yourself. Crack the husks of habit. Learn to poop without your phone. Fight to keep yourself engaged with other humans. Break things. Allow your brain to be broken. Unlearn something. Experience cow-like bliss. Experience the serenity of madness. Celebrate amateurism. Insist on the process of becoming. Retire the word “work.” Aesthetics as aisthesis. Practice being unlikable. Exhale.

Welcome to Earth Tones Museum!This page is a continuum, with more to be added and augmented. How to navigate:Click on any petroglyph to be taken to its corresponding page. The exhibits are on the largest stone and will “open” on set dates, so check back in! Every one of the six exhibits follows a theme, with specific maneuvering instructions for each page. To find the next exhibit search for the labeled portal that will take  you into the next room. The petroglyph of chairs will take you to our digital community center. If you would like to donate a work or interactive element to this center, please reach out. Indulge in pleasure!
